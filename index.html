What is this?

It's a soundboard! Turn on your volume, and press some buttons!

The sounds are taken from a Freedom of Information request to TransPennine Trains from 2023 and assembled by me – alifeee – for a wedding.

The sounds weren't well labelled by TransPennine Express. Sam ran them through automatic transcription software (whisper-cpp). Some of the transcriptions are not great ;]. If you can be bothered to fix any – or want to download all the 1,074 sounds – feel free to check out the website source.

I wish you well on your sound journey below and… thank you for travelling with TransPennine Express!
